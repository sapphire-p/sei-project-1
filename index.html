You play a park warden at Cretaceous Park, an impressive dinosaur park where the attractions bite back.

A fault with the security systems has caused the dinosaurs to break out of their enclosures - now they're making a dash for the exit!

Your mission: hop into your tank and bring those hungry dinos down before they make it to the gate.

Tap the left and right arrow keys to move the tank, and space bar to fire a missile

But watch out: those crafty dinos have learned to throw rocks, so you'll need to dodge those too!

Good luck!
