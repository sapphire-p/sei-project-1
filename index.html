You play a park warden at Lizard Land™, an impressive dinosaur park where the attractions bite back.

A security systems fault has caused the dinosaurs to break out of their enclosures - and now they're making a dash for the exit! Your mission: hop into your tank and bring those dinos down before they make it to the gate.

Tap the left and right arrow keys ←   → to move the tank, and space bar to fire a missile.

But watch out! Those crafty dinos have learned to throw rocks, so you'll need to dodge those too...Good luck!
